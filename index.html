delegate

The amount of ADA held by each user represents a stake in the network.
Delegating your ada held in a compatible wallet enables you to earn rewards
and allows your participation in the successful running of the network.

The most popular wallets with staking ability are Daedalus and Yoroi.
If you don't have ADA you can buy it using cryptocurrency exchanges(Bitpanda, Binance, Bittrex, etc.).
Learn more about Cardano delegation cycle.

Delegate to eden garden pool
