how to Delegate with eden garden

Delegating your ADA held in a compatible wallet enables you to earn rewards and allows your participation in the successful running of the network. The most popular wallets with staking ability are Daedalus and Yoroi. If you don't have ADA you can buy it using cryptocurrency exchanges (Bitpanda, Binance, Bittrex, etc.).

1.

Install

Download and install
Daedalus or Yoroi.

2.

Create

Create a wallet and send your funds to the wallet address.
3.

Delegate

Select "Stake pools" in Daedalus or "Delegate" in Yoroi. Search for EDEN, choose your Eden pool and delegate.

4.

Enjoy

Grow your wealth with
Eden Garden Pools!
